How?

Uploaded pictures are first scanned for faces by libccv, then if possible cropped so that the top and edge of the found faces are clipped. After that, it goes through some ImageMagick conversions to add camera shake and various camera-isn't-properly-configured effects. Then it's uploaded to imgur and a hosting .html file is generated to display the result.

Why?

I was bored and needed to play around with some technology. I wanted to gain familiarity with ImageMagick, Amazon EC2, form upload/download in node.js, and to test out some deployment mechanisms.
